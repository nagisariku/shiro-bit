### More Than Just Building Websites (`#more-than-building`)

- **Form**: Prose with blockquotes and styled lists.
- **Copywriting**:
  - "We don’t simply design visually appealing websites. We build strategic digital platforms that are engineered to help businesses grow, scale, and compete."
  - **Quote**: "The reality is, most websites fail—not because they look bad, but because they are not built to perform."
  - **Bullet Points**:
    - **They don't convert visitors into leads**: Beautiful pages with no clear path to action waste every click you earn.
    - **They are not structured to support marketing and traffic growth**: They lack the structural foundation and SEO integrity required to support and sustain traffic acquisition.
    - **They don’t contribute to long-term business success**: They operate as static digital brochures instead of scalable, long-term growth engines.
  - **Bottom Line**: "At ShiroBIT, we flip the script. Every UX decision, every line of code, and every SEO strategy is aligned for client-acquisition systems that work for you 24/7."
